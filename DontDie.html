Hold Aggro - Don't Die

The very core of tanking is built around this concept:
Get enemy aggro, keep it, and don't die along the way.
This is done through taunting, blocking, healing, and managing your resources.

What is taunting?

Taunting is a skill that will make an enemy focus their attacks on you.
In this game, taunting is binary. If you've taunted the enemy, it will target you.

What Skills Taunt?

There are exactly three skills in this game that taunt.

  • Puncture

    • This skill is available from the Sword and Shield skill line.
    It's short ranged, but is both cheap and strips armor. Every Tank Should Use This.

  • Inner Fire

    • This skill is available from the Undaunted skill line.
    This is a long range taunt. It's more expensive than puncture, but some fights will require you to have a long ranged taunt.

  • Destructive Clench

    • This skill is available from the Destruction Staff skill line, and must be morphed from Destructive Touch
    It's also a long ranged taunt, but is shorter than Inner Fire, more expensive, and requires you to use an ice staff for the skill to actually taunt.
    The only upside over Inner Fire is that this skill applies Major Maim, which decreases the damage the enemy does by 10%

Stamina Management

Stamina regeneration is set to zero while blocking.
As a result, you'll need to limit how many stamina skills you use and know when to block to conserve it.
Skills, passives, and synergies which directly restore stamina are far more helpful as a result.

Magicka Management

Don't overcast your healing!
Stack magicka regen enchants and use skills that restore magicka!
