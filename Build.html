Build Set Up

The right equipment can help you manage your resources better, or bring the utility you need to win the fight.
The general focus is on what kind of equipment you bring, what sets that equipment is made of, and the skills you put on your bars.

Equipment

Weapon Choice

  • One Hand And Shield

    • This weapon gives you access to the cheapest taunt in the game (plus more perks in 'debuffing').
    Additionally it gives lots of passives for damage reduction and stamina sustain.

  • Ice Staff

    • Access to one of the only two ranged taunts in the game, in addition to many utility options.
    It's passives are slightly less effective than a sword and board, but it's still defense oriented.

Armor Choice

  • 5 Heavy, 1 Medium, 1 Light

    • Higher max resources thanks to the Undaunted Mettle passive.

  • 7 Heavy

    • Much higher defensive stats and passives, but less max resources (aside from HP), and slightly less utility.

Skill Set Up

There are six skills that are necessary in every tank loadout.
A cheap taunt, access to minor maim, a self heal based on your max HP, a stamina tool, an armor skill, a ranged taunt, and a survival ultimate.
